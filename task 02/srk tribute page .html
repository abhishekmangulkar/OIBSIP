SHAH RUKH KHAN

About SRK

Shah Rukh Khan is an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality. He has appeared in more than 80 Bollywood films, and earned several awards for his roles, including 14 Filmfare Awards. He is one of the richest actors in the world, and has a net worth of over $600 million.

Khan began his career in the late 1980s, and gained widespread popularity in the 1990s, appearing in a series of romantic dramas such as Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge and Dil To Pagal Hai. He has since appeared in a variety of genres, including action films such as Don and Ra.One, and dramas such as Swades and Chak De! India.

Outside of acting, Khan has also been involved in philanthropic work. He has supported several charities and causes, and has been named a UNESCO Goodwill Ambassador for his efforts to promote child rights.
